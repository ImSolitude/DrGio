The road to excellence is invariably strewn with setbacks, obstacles, and adversity. It is one thing to make a commitment to achieve your goals, but it is quite another thing to persevere against the adversity, setbacks, and difficulties that stand in the way of achieving those goals.

The simple fact is that becoming great at golf will not be easy, and golfers have two choices to make. They can either hope that their road will be easy, or learn to think in ways that will prepare them to effectively deal with the obstacles inherent in the game. Dr. Valiante teaches his clients how to think in ways that give them the best chance to succeed at a difficult game, sometimes against long odds.
